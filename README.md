# THE INTERVAL OF CRACKING

🔗 **Live Preview**  
[Project Page](https://tanadon03.github.io/Reflection/)

---

A reflective web project exploring the space between an old self and a new becoming.

This work presents a short reflection divided into three parts:

- **Review** – thoughts from a classroom discussion about *มรณังสติ* (mindfulness of death)
- **Reflect** – an exploration of the metaphor of **molting**, the shedding of an old identity
- **Reflexivity** – questioning whether inner calmness comes from growth or from emotional defense

The project focuses on minimal presentation and atmosphere through a simple webpage format.
